Tall and Creamy Cheesecake

Description

A tall and creamy cheesecake that can be customized with whatever you like such as fruit, nuts or chocolate.

Takes 3 and a half hours to prepare and at least 4 hours of refrigeration.

Ingredients

FOR THE CRUST

  • 13⁄4 Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons of Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1⁄2 stick of unsalted Butter

FOR THE CHEESECAKE

  • 2 pounds of Cream Cheese
  • 11⁄3 cups of Sugar
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 teaspoons of Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 4 large Eggs
  • 11⁄3 cups of Sour Cream or Heavy Cream
